Customs Clearance Services

Bunker Logistics & Services SA provides efficient and reliable customs clearance services at all ports of entry in the country, streamlining import and export processes to enhance our clients’ operational efficiency.

Bunker Logistics & Services SA is a leading provider of efficient and reliable customs clearance services at all ports of entry in Mozambique. With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we specialize in streamlining import and export processes to enhance our clients’ operational efficiency and facilitate seamless international trade transactions.

Our Services

Import Clearance: We understand the complexities involved in importing goods into Mozambique. Our experienced team of customs clearance specialists works diligently to ensure the smooth and timely clearance of imported goods through customs procedures. From documentation preparation to customs declaration and duty assessment, we handle all aspects of the import clearance process with efficiency and accuracy.

Export Clearance: Exporting goods requires careful attention to compliance requirements and documentation. Our customs clearance services extend to export shipments, where we assist our clients in navigating export regulations, completing export declarations, and obtaining necessary permits and licenses. With our expertise in export procedures, we help streamline the export clearance process and ensure compliance with international trade regulations.

Tariff Classification and Valuation: Determining the correct tariff classification and valuation of goods is essential for accurate customs clearance and duty assessment. Our team provides expert guidance on tariff classification and valuation methodologies, helping our clients minimize customs duties and avoid potential penalties. By ensuring compliance with customs regulations, we help our clients optimize their import and export costs.

Duty Optimization: Customs duties can have a significant impact on the cost of international trade. Our customs clearance services include duty optimization strategies aimed at minimizing duty liabilities and maximizing cost savings for our clients. Through careful planning and utilization of duty relief programs and preferential trade agreements, we help our clients reduce their customs duties and improve their competitiveness in the global marketplace.

Proactive Communication
