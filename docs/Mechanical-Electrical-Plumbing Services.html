Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing Services

Our proficient team delivers expert mechanical, electrical, and plumbing services, ensuring the operational integrity and maintenance of our clients’ facilities.

At Bunker Logistics and Services SA, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive solutions to meet the diverse needs of our clients in Mozambique. With a focus on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we specialize in delivering expert mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) services to ensure the operational integrity and maintenance of our clients’ facilities.

Our Services

Mechanical Services: Our mechanical services encompass a wide range of solutions aimed at maintaining the functionality and efficiency of mechanical systems within our clients’ facilities. From HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning) systems to refrigeration units and industrial machinery, our proficient team of technicians is equipped to handle all aspects of mechanical maintenance, repair, and installation.

Electrical Services: Electrical systems are integral to the operation of any facility. Our electrical services cover everything from routine maintenance and troubleshooting to installation and upgrades of electrical infrastructure. Our certified electricians ensure the safety, reliability, and compliance of electrical systems, helping our clients minimize downtime and optimize energy efficiency.

Plumbing Services: Plumbing issues can disrupt operations and compromise the safety and comfort of occupants. Our plumbing services address a wide range of plumbing needs, including repairs, installations, and preventative maintenance. Whether it’s fixing leaks, clearing blockages, or installing new fixtures, our skilled plumbers provide prompt and efficient solutions to keep our clients’ facilities running smoothly.
